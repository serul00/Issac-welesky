About Me

Welcome to the enigmatic world of Isaac Welensky, the mystery composer whose music has captivated audiences from the grand halls of Vienna to the secretive speakeasies of New York City. Despite numerous attempts, no one has ever seen his face or heard his voice. Rumor has it that he once composed an entire symphony using only the sounds of his cat meowing. Or was it just a cat’s whim? The truth remains hidden, wrapped in the echoes of his timeless melodies.

Where to Find My Work

Isaac’s compositions can be found in the most unexpected places. Whether it’s a secretive music box in a quaint antique shop or a hidden playlist under the name “Mystic Melodies,” the search is part of the adventure. Keep your ears sharp and your curiosity sharper!
